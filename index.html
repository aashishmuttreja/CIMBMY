Redeem your Bonus Miles easily with any of these methods:

Get automatic cash rebate offers with Mastercard Traveler Rewards

Enjoy exclusive, automatic cash rebate offers from participating overseas merchants when you travel, shop and pay with your CIMB Enrich Platinum Credit Card. There are no coupons to carry, and no separate program membership card. Simply pay with your CIMB Enrich Platinum Credit Card using the currency shown in the offer and you will receive the advertised cashback offer amount.

 

How it works:

1. Travel to any of the international Mastercard Traveler Rewards destinations.

2. Shop with participating merchants and pay with your CIMB Enrich Platinum Credit Card.

3. Enjoy automatic cash rebates on eligible purchases, processed as a credit on your next credit card statement.
